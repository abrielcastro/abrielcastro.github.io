Three Types of Dogs

Bulldog

Bulldogs are a medium-sized breed of dog characterized by a wrinkled face and pushed-in nose. Bulldogs are gentle companions known for their affectionate nature.

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog known for its friendly attitude. They are one of the most popular breeds in the United States. Golden Retrievers are often used as guide dogs for the blind, search and rescue dogs, and in other roles.

Siberian Husky

Siberian Huskys are a medium-sized working dog breed known for their thick double coat and striking blue or multicolored eyes. Huskies are energetic and athletic dogs.
